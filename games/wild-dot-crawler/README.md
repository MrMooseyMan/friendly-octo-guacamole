## Strategy Tips

- **Long chains are powerful!** 5+ runes give 50% bonus to effects
- **Combo multiplier stacks** - Try to make all 3 moves quickly for max damage
- **Shield protects you** from enemy attacks - build it up before your turn ends
- **Poison is powerful** against tough enemies - it damages them every turn
- **Wild Surge** auto-triggers when energy is full - time it with combos for massive damage!
- **White runes are versatile** - damage, shield, AND energy in one move
